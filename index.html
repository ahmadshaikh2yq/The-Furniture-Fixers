What We Offer

Premium Carpentry & Design Services

Furniture Repair & Restoration
Breathe new life into your old or damaged furniture. We repair cracks, broken joints, broken legs, and restore finishes to perfection.
  • Chair & table joint repair
  • Wardrobe hinge & slider fixing
  • Wood crack filling & sealing
  • Polish & lacquer restoration
  • Antique furniture restoration
  • Termite damage repair
Custom Furniture Making
Bespoke furniture crafted to your exact dimensions and design preferences — every piece is unique and built to last generations.
  • Bed frames & headboards
  • Wardrobes & almirahs
  • Study & office tables
  • TV units & wall panels
  • Bookshelf & display units
  • Sofa frames & centre tables
Modular Kitchen
Transform your kitchen with fully modular designs — functional, beautiful and built to suit your cooking style and space requirements.
  • L-shape, U-shape, Parallel designs
  • Soft-close drawers & shutters
  • Loft & overhead cabinets
  • Chimney & appliance integration
  • Granite & quartz countertops
  • Modular accessories & fittings
Interior Design
End-to-end interior design services for homes, offices and commercial spaces — from concept to completed, move-in ready spaces.
  • Full home interiors
  • Office & commercial spaces
  • False ceiling (POP & gypsum)
  • Flooring (wooden, tile, vinyl)
  • Wall paneling & wallpaper
  • Lighting design & installation
Doors, Windows & Partitions
Custom wooden doors, window frames, and glass partitions manufactured and installed for any residential or commercial requirement.
  • Flush & panel door making
  • Sliding & folding doors
  • Window frame repair & making
  • Glass & wooden partitions
  • Pocket & barn door systems
  • Fire-rated doors supply
Upholstery & Polishing
Complete upholstery re-doing and furniture polishing services — bring back the showroom look to your old sofas, chairs and wood pieces.
  • Sofa upholstery re-doing
  • Chair & bench padding
  • Headboard upholstery
  • Wood polish (matte, gloss, satin)
  • Veneer & laminate application
  • Duco paint & enamel finish

Our Story

Built on Craft & Trust

The Furniture Fixers was founded with a simple mission: to deliver carpentry and furniture services that people in Mumbai could truly trust. What started as a small repair workshop has grown into a full-service carpentry and interior design company serving hundreds of happy homes.

Under the leadership of our founder — a master craftsman with over 20 years of experience — our team has built a reputation for quality that no other furniture service in Mumbai can match.

Today, we are a team of 50+ skilled carpenters, designers and project managers dedicated to turning your vision into reality.
